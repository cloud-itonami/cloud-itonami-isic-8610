What this is

A hospital actor for inpatient-admission intake, jurisdiction assessment, credential screening, treatment administration, and discharge authorization. The HospitalOps-LLM proposes clinical actions, but an independent Clinical Oversight Governor validates every decision against jurisdiction-specific regulations and clinical evidence — and a human licensed clinician always approves treatment and discharge.

Built on a governed, spec-cited, audited execution scaffold so any qualified hospital operator can fork this repo, deploy it, run it, and extend it without building the compliance layer from scratch.

This is not just code: it's a complete open business model including governance, trust controls, and an operator playbook for self-hosting in your jurisdiction.

Core capabilities

  • Admission intake: Patient registration and initial triage
  • Jurisdiction assessment: Compliance with hospital-institution licensing requirements (spec-cited per jurisdiction)
  • Credential screening: Verification that treating clinician licenses are current
  • Treatment administration: LLM-drafted proposals with human sign-off (never autonomous)
  • Discharge authorization: Post-procedure observation verification with human approval (never autonomous)
  • Immutable audit ledger: Every intake, assessment, screening, and actuation decision is logged

Trust & safety

  • No treatment is administered and no discharge is authorized without human clinician sign-off
  • Fabricated jurisdiction citations, incomplete clinical evidence, insufficient observation periods, and stale clinician licenses each force a hold — never an override
  • Double administration and double discharge guards prevent repeated actions on the same admission
  • Patient health data stays outside Git; emergency manual overrides remain outside LLM control
